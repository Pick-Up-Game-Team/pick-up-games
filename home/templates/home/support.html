General

What is a ‘Pick Up Game’ ?
A pick-up game is when a group of players get together and spontaneously start a game. Players usually know where and when to show up to a basketball court to play pick up basketball. You can play one game or a hundred games, there is no commitment to stay. You can come and go as you please.
What are the supported sports in the web application ?
Currenty, we only support soccer, tennis, baseball, and especially basketball.
What are the rules ?
Unlike official games, some pick up games will have unwritten rules. Each sport and each court will have its own rule. See Sports and Courts for more information.
